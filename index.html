About the Artist

"Meet Matthew Rivera, a young tattoo artist who embarked on his creative journey just under a year ago. However, his passion for art has been ingrained in him since childhood. With a natural talent for drawing that bloomed early on, Matthew's dedication and hard work have transformed him into the remarkable artist he is today. Each stroke of his needle is a testament to his unwavering commitment to his craft. Through his artistry, Matthew seeks to not only create stunning tattoos but also to share his passion and creativity with each of his clients, leaving a lasting impression one design at a time."
